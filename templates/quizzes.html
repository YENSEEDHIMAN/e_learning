Welcome to the Quiz Section

Our quiz section is designed to test your knowledge and help reinforce your learning. Each course has its own quiz, tailored to match the topics and difficulty levels you’ve studied.

Quizzes are a great way to practice, learn, and even earn certificates! They include multiple choice questions based on your selected course.

How to Start a Quiz


  • Go to the Courses page and select your preferred course.
  • Click on the “Start Quiz” button available in the course description.
  • Each quiz consists of 10–15 questions and is time-limited.

Difficulty Levels


  • Beginner: Basic questions to test foundational understanding.
  • Intermediate: More detailed logic and practical problem-solving.
  • Advanced: Real-world application-based and challenging problems.

Certificate

Once you complete a quiz with a good score, a certificate of completion is generated automatically. You can download and share it with your network or include it in your resume!
